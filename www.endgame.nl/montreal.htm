MONTREAL 1979

pgn-file of tournaments (34 games)     zip-file of CBase-file of tournaments

Super tournaments with Soviet participation became regular events in the mid seventies. Kavalek, assisted by the movie makers Passer and Forman, organised the "Tournament of Stars". An amount of $110,000 became available for Montreal 1979. Ten Grandmasters stayed in the Meridien Hotel and played in the Quebec Pavilion from 10 iv until 6 v. The inactive Fischer and rebellious Korchnoi were not among them. The games of the winners Karpov and Tal have been selected. Karpov was world champion at that time. Tal had a period of good health. It allowed him to play his best chess.
