The first VAM tournament was played at the town hall of Hoogeveen in October 1997. Principle organizer was the major Sytze Faber. Chess players participated in various groups. We will only follow the top.
Each year four persons took part in the Crown Group: a former world champion, the best female of the world, the strongest Dutch player and the youth world champion. This formula became less strict later. Double rounds were carried out. Glass pieces were used. Kortschnoj did not break them during time pressure. All tournaments were played in October. When two players ended at the first place, a tie-break decided about victory. The winner got a special glass chess set each year. Essent became the sponsor in 2000. Univé overtook the sponsorship in 2009. Polgar has won the Crown Group three times; I.Sokolov, Mamedyarov and Tiviakov twice.

The Crown Group event was replaced by two matches in 2014. Two young stars crushed old hands.
