DOS HERMANAS 1992-99

Jan van Reek

pgn-file of the events     zip of CBase-files

 

Dos Hermanas (two sisters) is situated near Sevilla. A series of annual chess contests started in the spring of 1989. It became a super event in 1992.

Ten top players starred from 21 v until 1 vi 1996 in Hotel La Motilla. Kramnik became winner on the Sonneborn-Berger score. Topalov joined the lead in points. Anand and Kasparov shared the third place. 

The last super tournament happened in 1999. Hereafter, internet competitions were organised in Dos Hermanas.
