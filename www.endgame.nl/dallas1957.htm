DALLAS 1957

Dallas 1957 was played from 30 xi until 16 xii. The main event was a contest between eight players. Evans, Larsen and Olafsson were the young talents. Reshevsky, Gligoric, Szabo, Najdorf and Yanofsky had more experience. Bronstein got no visa for a visit to Texas. Pal Benko defeated Ken Smith in a match, as side event. Games were played in Hotel Adolphus, the highest building of Texas. The time limit was 40 moves in two hours, followed by 20 moves in one hour. A bulletin was published for each round. Kashdan functioned as commentator.
