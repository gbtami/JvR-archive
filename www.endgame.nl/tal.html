THE TAL MEMORIAL

Mikhail Tal and his hypnotic stare


pgn annotated games of Tal Memorial

Mikhail Tal lived from 9 xi 1936 until 28 vi 1992. He had become world champion in his great attacking style. A memorial tournament was held in his hometown Riga from 12 until 24 iv 1995.
A new Tal Memorial was played in Moscow's Central Chess Club from 6 until 16 xi 2006. The prize fund was $100,000. It became the start of an annual event. Top-twenty players participated mostly.

Winners Tal Memorial:
Riga 1995 (0): Kasparov
Moscow 2006 (1): Leko
Moscow 2007 (2): Kramnik
Moscow 2008 (3): Ivanchuk

Moscow 2009 (4): Kramnik

Moscow 2010 (5): Aronian and Karjakin

Moscow 2011 (6): Carlsen

Moscow 2012 (7): Carlsen

Moscow 2013 (8): Gelfand
