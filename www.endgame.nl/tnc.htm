New Chess came into being by the professionalisation of the game. Characteristics are thorough openings’ preparation and support by seconds. Computer analysis was added later. It strongly increased the level of over-the-board chess. The approach is best known from the struggle for the initiative by the attackers Alekhine, Botvinnik and Kasparov, but the preparation by the prophylactic players Fischer, Karpov and Kramnik fares very similar. 

However, I drew a surprising conclusion when I analysed the games of Linares 2003 after the perusal of a correspondence chess world championship. Exceptional mistakes were made in the rook ending Lékó - Anand. The technical level was obviously higher in correspondence chess. New grandmaster draws arise in o-t-b chess, when complicated lines are prepared and games end peacefully after short tactics.  

An enormous development has been made in correspondence chess during the last decades, indeed. Nowadays, the means are similar to o-t-b chess, because computers are extensively used and sometimes seconds, friends and lovers assist the players. Everything has become novel: preparation, tactics, planning and length of game. Still many encounters are decided. 

Correspondence games are little known to the general public. Therefore, I present fragments from six outstanding games. Basic strategic plans are shown at the highest level. The winners of the games Van Oosterom, Elwert, Umansky and Timmerman are first-rate representatives of Novel Chess.

Counterattack

 

13. Nf2! The exchange 13. Nxc5 bxc5 gains little (Umansky). 13. .. g5!? 14. Be3 f5?! My preference goes to 14. .. Ng6! 15. Rc1 (Umansky), and now 15. .. Ne5! 15. Bd4 Bxd4 16. Qxd4 f4 17. b4! f3?
